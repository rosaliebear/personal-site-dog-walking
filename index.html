ACTIVITIES
Excercise
Fun and Games
Brisk Walking

Brisk Walking is a great way to start off an excercise routine for dogs who don't get much excercise and are just starting out.


Light Jogging

Light jogging is agreat option for active dogs who are ready for some intermediate excercise but nothing too heavy.


Intermediate Jogging

Intermediate jogging includes all the intensity of jogging with the ease of brisk walking.


Running and Sprinting

Running and sprinting are our highest level excercise routine for dogs who are extremely active and can handle a fast paced jog.


Frisbee

Frisbee being one of our most popular activities allows dogs to let all that energy loose and catch frisbees in the air!


Fetch

Fetch is a classic game of throwing a ball and the dog running to get it and bring it back.


Scavenger Hunt

Scavenger Hunt is a fun game played with 2+ dogs in which objects are hidden around the park and dogs have to find them.
